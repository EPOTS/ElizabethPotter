More about the Decadent NYC Passport

Passport Description

Chocalatiers will be picked up by our Decadent Chocalate Van in front of Chelsea Market,75 9th Avenue, New York. Pick Up Times will be Saturday 11am, 12pm & 5pm, Sunday 12pm & 4pm. The duration of the tour is 2 hrs (approximately). The final Destination will be Mast Brothers Chocolate Factory, 111 N 3rd St., Broooklyn

Tour Info & Capacity

  1. 20 persons per guide
  2. Food is included
  3. 5 tastings on the go (sidewalk tastings,or done inside the establishments)

Reccomended Supplies

How to take the Decadent Chocolate Tours

Tickets must be purchased in advance, and tours sell out quickly. To maximize your chances of getting the day and time you prefer, please book well in advance. Tickets cana only be purchased with a Visa, Mastercard or American Express. We offer private tours and we can accomodate a variety of special requests.
