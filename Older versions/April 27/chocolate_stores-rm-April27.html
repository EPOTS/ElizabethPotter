NYC Chocolatiers

Li-Lac Chocolates

40 8th Ave., New York, NY (212) 924-2280

Li-Lac Chocolates

Come step inside Li-Lac Chocolates, it is one of oldest chocolate houses in Manhattan. Li-Lac Chocolates has been delighting its visitors with their small batches of mouthwatering, handmade treats of chocolate, since 1923. You will surely be delighted with Li-Lac's old fashion charm and the exquisite smells and tastes of these chocolates.

Serendipity 3

225 East 60th St., New York, NY (212) 838-3531

Serendipity 3

Serendipity is definitely a New York Chocolate Must, it was established in 1954. Many famous people have frequenteed this chocolate haven, such as Marilyn Monroe & the First Lady Jackie O., enjoying every morsel of chocolate. When Serendipity celebrated it's 50th Anniversary they introduced the Golden Opulence Sundae which in the Guiness Book of World Records registered as the most expensive dessert, at a whopping $1000. One of the favorites is the Frozen Hot Chocolate, will definitely be worth the trip.

Mast Brothers

111 N. 3rd St., Brooklyn, NY (718) 388-2625

Mast Brothers Chocolate Factory

Visit & learn how chocolate is made from bean to bar. The flavors found in this place are really unique comparable to fine wine. Chocolatiers will be able to enjoy 5 chocolates discovering how the different beans yield to the mind blogging delicious flavors.
